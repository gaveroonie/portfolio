Project brief

A page to view what speakers are speaking at the event.

Must show details about speaker including name, job title, company & and speaker bio. Must use existing database of speaker images. Speaker images are generally pretty low quality and taken from LinkedIn.

Original Site

Speaker cards

Speaker card requirements were to include photo and logo. Putting these two elements together on a single card always made the card look crowded, but the displaying the logo was the easiest way to advertise if a speaker came from a recognisable company, which was a huge selling point for our events so was non-negotiable.

Cards Low fidelity

The logo along with the speaker photo never really sat well with me but was a necessity to advertise the big named companies, and originally we only displayed the logo which felt a bit impersonal, so was a huge improvement on what we had.

Without the logo the card is definitely a lot cleaner, but the brief required the logo.
